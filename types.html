Mini Drones

Small, but hard to ignore are the following mini drones that are sure to impress one and all with their amazing capabilities despite their dainty image and humble proportions. Below is a compilation of five of the best mini drones, with ratings based on their features and specifications.

The Parrot comes with a GPS and a Return Home button. It has a 14 megapixel camera that comes with a 180 degree view and fish-eye lens, and provides a 1080 pixel full HD resolution. Weighing barely 410 grams, it measures 330 x 380 x 36 mm, which we can say is pretty compact to carry around snugly. Its battery has a flight time capacity of 11 minutes, depending on wind velocity. Parrot’s FreeFlight app can be used with this mini drone to control it using smartphones and tablets. It also comes with Skycontroller that enables it connect wirelessly to smartphones for FPV flights. Definitely a small but feature-packed devil.

Scores high on ease of use, battery life, and navigation control, this baby is suited for amateurs and hobbyists alike. Comes armed with a 14 megapixel camera and CMOS sensor that can capture video at 1080 pixels at 30fps. The camera also features a 90 degree angle The Phantom comes with a three-axial gimbal that offers greater stability and smoother video capture even in jerky situations. It offers real view of what the camera records by connecting it to smartphone using DJI’s Vision app. The mini drone contains a 5200mAh Li-Po battery, which provides a flight time of 25 minutes which is a great deal for a mini drone. The drone weighs 1284 grams. Comes with GPS and WiFi extender.

Hubsan X4 H107D

This is one of the most compact and handy piece of work. Its USP is the FPV or the First-Person-View that enables real time viewing on the controller, while allowing to capture the video on memory card. It comes with a 0.3 megapixel camera with a 640 X 480 resolution. It contains a 3.7-volt 380-mAh lithium polymer battery and a full charge lasts for 7 minutes while on the fly. It is operable for a range of a good 328 ft. It weighs 360 grams and measures 102 x 102 x 51 mm. In a nutshell, this mini flyer is efficient for shorter flights and recordings. Nano QX RTF with SAFE

The Nano is a quite drone and make its trips unannounced. Its direct drive propellers ensure a quite flight. It contains E-flite® 1S 3.7V 150mAh 25C Li-Po Battery which lasts around 5 minutes of flight time. A great option to carry while being outdoors.

This mini beauty showcases 3-axis gyro stabilization system that lends stability to this mini machine. Easy to setup and ultra-easy to handle, this drone comes in handy for first-timers. It features a Devo F4 FPV transmitter with 3.5″ LCD screen and a 5.8G FPV camera. It weighs less than 40 grams and measures 125 x 38 mm. Long Range Drones

Personal radio-controlled drones are becoming exceedingly common in today’s market. For hobbyists and newcomers alike, drones offer a unique new way to approach RC flight and allow users to capture sights that weren’t possible on their own. So, it’s no wonder every other drone comes with a payload capacity or ability to attach a GoPro camera to its frame . Video and photographs from previously impossible angles are created, meaning there’s more incentive for enthusiasts beyond piloting a small machine.

Beyond how long they can remain in the sky, however, drones do have some limitations. Finding out which drone in which price tier has the least amount of limitations is the key to enjoying the hobby to its fullest. In this case, it’s about range. Since drones are remote controlled they need a signal to respond to from the pilot’s point. Good drones can still hold steady hundreds, if not thousands of feet, away. Lesser models will likely falter after about a few hundred feet or less.

DJI Phantom 3 Quadcopter (Standard version)

DJI and its respective fleet often find their units on our list for having a pretty lengthy variety of models to choose from. That, and the hardware in question has a lot to offer. Previously the Phantom 3 was also on our list of drones with the longest battery life, because it sported a whopping 20 minutes. Let’s add to that, as the Phantom 3 has been recorded to hit distances of about 500 meters, or 1500 feet. That manes enthusiasts can get some really amazing sky shots while the Phantom 3 does its thing. The basic unit, which lacks an extra batter and such, sits close to $500 USD, making it a decent long range quadcopter for sale in this tier.

If the name didn’t already imply it, the designers really want you to understand the “X” is a major feature here. Even the drone has an x like shape. More to the point, as a long range drone with camera, and it’s heavy $560 USD pricetag, one expects some performance. Well, it does deliver at least in principle, with a bulky sky life of 20 minutes. But, emphasizing flight range, our x-loving drone gets around on 1800 feet ranges, or 600 meters. From there, you can capture all its sported 1080p video you want. Xiro Xplorer V is FPV drone by all means. Video transmission works very well, better than I expected to be honest. There’s updated version of this drone available now (Xiro Xplorer 2) which looks very futuristic. That also means this version is now on sale, so get your piece right now!

Blade Chroma

This great looking drone deserved it’s place on this list. It’s not only one of the drones with best flight range in this price range but it’s also one of the best drones for aerial photography and videography in this price range because of it’s stability, reliability and SAFE technology Blade putted into it. Using solely a remote control for operation, the Chroma can be flown up to 400 meters (1,312 feet) away from you. Compared to the average controllable range of 100 meters, you can control the Chroma from 300 meters (984 feet) further away. That’s not where the party ends. There are several versions of Chroma, some of them supports GoPro Hero 3 and 4 action cameras, while one version(on the picture) comes with it’s own camera. Both are great quality but buying one that works with GoPro can save you some money in case you already have GoPro camera.
